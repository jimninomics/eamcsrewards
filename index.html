Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Season Field Pass?

The Season Field Pass is your path to rewards. By playing games, completing challenges, and earning XP, you’ll progress through levels to unlock exclusive items, player cards, packs, and more.

How do I claim my rewards?

Rewards are automatically added to your Ultimate Team binder once you hit the required milestone on the Field Pass. Some special rewards, like Welcome Packs or Fantasy Packs, will appear in your unopened packs section.

What are Campus Heroes?

Campus Heroes are players who were college football stars and are now making an impact in the NFL. When you add two Campus Heroes who were once teammates, you’ll unlock special chemistry boosts for your roster.

 

Do I need to purchase the Field Pass?

No purchase is required. The Field Pass is free for all players, though some bonus rewards may be tied to premium content or limited-time promotions.

 

Can I earn rewards just by logging in?

Yes! For Season 1, you’ll get a Helmet Stickers player just by logging in for the first time, plus other exclusive bonuses through themed events.

 

What are House Rules events?

House Rules are special limited-time game modes with unique rulesets. Compete in these challenges to earn exclusive rewards and progress faster in the Field Pass.
